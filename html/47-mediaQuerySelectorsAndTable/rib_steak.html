Smokie's Grill


At Smokie's, we pride ourselves on the quality of our materials. Our cows are grass-fed American bred, and we pay top dollar so our customers get the meal they deserve. The recipe for our famous Rib Steak was invented in 1987 by our long standing chef, Emeril Lagasse. The special blend was culmination of several years tinkering and experimenting in the kitchen. While it has a strong French flavor, experiences tasters will detect an hint of Italian seasoning.
